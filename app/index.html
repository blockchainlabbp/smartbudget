What is smartbudget?

Smartbudget is a Ethereum Smart Contract-based decentralised application, which allows you to coordinate projects funded in ETH, from the comfort of your browser.

Let's say you want to build a cool website. You know how to deal with the backend, but you aren't a great designer. So why don't you hire someone for that part?

With SmartBudget, you can create a smart contract which

  • will host your tasks (a.k.a. subprojects) where freelancers and contractors can find them
  • ensures your project budget will be locked until a delivery lock time chosen by you - this will let contractors know you cannot spend the allocateds funds on something else
  • lets you choose from the contractors applying for your subprojects
  • allows you to pay your contractors in ETH with a simple click, when the end product is delivered

Since each of the above steps are recorded on the Ethereum blockchain, your whole project is extremely transparent, fair and easy to manage.

Interested? Try it out yourself! First make sure you have MetaMask installed and you have logged in to one of the networks with an account.
